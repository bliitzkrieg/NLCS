Why Should You Seal Stamped and Exposed Aggregate Concrete?

Whether you have beautiful new or old stamped or exposed aggregate patio, walkway, driveway or pool decks, its important to protect your investment.

The right sealer will keep your concrete looking great for many years, or rejuvenate an old existing stamped or exposed aggregate surface.

A sealer will improve the appearance of stamped concrete, prevent fading caused by UV rays and protect from stains and harmful chemicals.

Your stamped concrete patio, driveway or walkway will be subjected to many things that can leave stains on the surface such as oil, salt, chemicals, leaves, dirt, fertilizer and more. The right sealer will keep all of these from penetrating the surface of the concrete and leaving an unsightly stain.

You live in a cold climate and by re-sealing your concrete, you reduce the chance of freeze thaw damage. Freeze thaw damage can cause cracks or surface flaking.

Finally a sealer will keep water from sinking into your concrete. When properly re-sealed the service life of concrete is greatly extended!
